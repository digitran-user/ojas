Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about going solar in Odisha

A typical 3 kW residential system costs ₹1.5–1.8 lakh before subsidy. After the PM Surya Ghar subsidy of ₹78,000, your net cost is often under ₹1 lakh. Payback period is 4–6 years, after which you generate free electricity for 20+ years.
PM Surya Ghar provides subsidies up to ₹78,000 for residential solar installations: ₹30,000/kW for the first 2 kW and ₹18,000/kW for the 3rd kW. We process all paperwork and you receive funds directly in your bank account within 30-45 days.
We complete most residential installations in 1–3 days. This includes panel mounting, inverter installation, net-metering connection, and app setup. Commercial and large systems may take 5–7 days. We don't cut corners!
Our grid-tied systems work when the grid is on, generating and selling surplus power. For backup during cuts, we offer battery solutions (Li-ion/LiFePO4). Odisha has ~300 sunny days/year—even on cloudy monsoon days, panels generate 30-50% capacity.
Solar panels are practically maintenance-free! Just wash them every 1–2 months to remove dust. We offer Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC) starting at ₹2,500/year that include 2 professional cleaning visits and remote monitoring of your system performance.
